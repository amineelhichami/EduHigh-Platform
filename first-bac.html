1st Year Baccalaureate

Build your English foundation. Prepare for the regional exam with 8 thematic units.

See units ↓

Core Skills You'll Develop

🔤 Grammar

Simple past, past continuous, present perfect, comparatives, future forms.

🗣️ Speaking and Writing

Describing experiences, writing short narratives, expressing preferences.

📖 Reading

Understanding short articles, stories, and everyday texts.

8 Thematic Units (Regional Exam Focus)

  • Unit 1: Personal Introductions – Greetings, verb 'to be', describing people
  • Unit 2: Daily Routines – Present simple, adverbs of frequency
  • Unit 3: My Hometown – Past simple of 'to be', places vocabulary
  • Unit 4: Food and Health – Count/uncount nouns, quantifiers, recipes
  • Unit 5: Celebrations – Past continuous, time clauses (when/while)
  • Unit 6: Future Plans – Going to vs. will, making predictions
  • Unit 7: Education in Morocco – Comparatives/superlatives, school subjects
  • Unit 8: Review and Regional Exam Practice – Mock test + writing workshop

Why First Year Matters

The regional exam at the end of this year tests your reading, grammar, and writing. Our exercises are designed to give you confidence and a strong base for the final baccalaureate year.
