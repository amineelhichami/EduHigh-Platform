10 Complete Units for the Bac Exam

  • Unit 1: Youth and Citizenship – Vocabulary, phrasal verbs, present perfect
  • Unit 2: Cultural Values – Modals, expressing opinion, idioms
  • Unit 3: Education – Passive voice, essay writing (for/against)
  • Unit 4: Technology and Innovation – Future tenses, compound adjectives
  • Unit 5: Women and Power – Reported speech, linking words
  • Unit 6: Environmental Issues – Conditional sentences, cause/effect writing
  • Unit 7: Health andWelfare – Vocabulary of illness, advice structures
  • Unit 8: Media and Communication – Relative clauses, formal letters
  • Unit 9: Economy andDevelopment – Gerunds/infinitives, data commentary
  • Unit 10: Review and Mock Exam – Full test simulation + strategies
