What You'll Learn

Introduce yourself, ask questions, describe your family and hobbies.

✍️ Writing Sentences

From words to simple paragraphs - describe a day or a friend.

Core Learning Units - Complete Syllabus

  • Unit 1: Personal Relations

    You learn how to introduce yourself, talk about your family, and use basic grammar like “to be”, pronouns, and nationalities.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 2: Education

    You learn vocabulary about school, studies, teachers, and how to talk about your education and daily school life.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 3: Food

    You learn food vocabulary, how to order in a restaurant, and grammar like some/any, much/many, and quantities (a little/a few).
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 4: Shopping

    You learn about clothes, stores, comparing things, expressing preferences, and using present continuous to talk about actions happening now.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 5: Recreation

    You learn about free time activities, hobbies, TV programs, expressing likes/dislikes, and talking about the future using “will” and “going to”.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 6: Health

    You learn body parts, health problems, giving advice (must/have to), asking permission (can/may), and basic conditionals.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 7: Arts

    You learn about artists, music, movies, and how to use past simple (regular + irregular verbs) to talk about past events.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 8: Science & Technology

    You learn about inventions, scientists, computers, gadgets, and past continuous to describe actions happening in the past.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 9: Environment & Ecology

    You learn about pollution, nature problems, present perfect tense, and how to talk about environmental issues and solutions.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

  • Unit 10: Economy

    You learn money vocabulary, shopping and business ideas, and how economy works in simple terms.
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Reading
    • Writing

Each topic includes vocabulary flashcards, interactive exercises, and a mini quiz to track your progress.
