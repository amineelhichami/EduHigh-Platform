6 Fun and Engaging Topics

  • Topic 1: All About Me – Subject pronouns, verb 'to be', nationalities
  • Topic 2: My Family – Possessive adjectives, family vocabulary, simple present
  • Topic 3: My Daily Routine – Time expressions, present simple negatives/questions
  • Topic 4: Food and Shopping – Like/don't like, countable/uncountable, prices
  • Topic 5: My Town – There is/are, prepositions of place, directions
  • Topic 6: Holidays and Weather – Present continuous vs. present simple, seasons

Each topic includes vocabulary flashcards, interactive exercises, and a mini quiz to track your progress.
